About Us

Our Story

Avenir Ghana Limited, headquartered in Accra Ghana, is a digital marketing, website development, and creative web design company in Ghana. We provide large companies as well as small businesses and start-ups with services in building clean and accessible websites and web applications.
Our goal is to exceed the expectations of our clients and to ensure that our services provide optimal results in any business or undertaking.

QKnow

Education

Quick Knowledge (QKnow) is a Ghanaian education software created by Avenir to guide learners from Primary 1 and beyond. QKnow offers books, articles, practice exercises, quizzes, and a community focused learning environment. It tackles mathematics, science, english, social studies, and more.
